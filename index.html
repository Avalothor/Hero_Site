Brief info about Atatürk:

Mustafa Kemal Atatürk (alternatively Mustafa Kemal Paşa until 1934, commonly referred as Atatürk; 1881-10 November 1938) was a field marshal, leader of Turkish National Movement, revolutionary statesman, author, founder, and first president of the Republic of Turkey until his death (1923-1938). He modernized the Turkey into a secular and industrial nation with progressive reforms, based on his policies and theories, also known as Kemalism. Also encouraged the adoption of European way of life which is more modern than Eastern-Islamic way of life at that time. His vision is a modern Turkey than its predecessor at the moment, crumbling Ottoman Empire and its backward customs.

Due to his military and political accomplishments, Atatürk is regarded as one of the most important political leaders of the 20th century.
